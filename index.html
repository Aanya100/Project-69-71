My school has a lot of greenery and it is beautiful.

I had took admission in this school in 2016.

The vision of the school is to create and nurture a learning community of students, teachers and parents – a community that believes in the possibility of progressive development of children and adults through change in education. Each child is recognised for her own strengths, creating an environment free of judgement and conformity.



I believe all beings inherently love to learn. Children learn by sharing their discoveries with each other, their teachers and parents.

We believe in the philosophy of inclusion and facilitate an inclusive environment for children with varying abilities and from different socio-economic backgrounds.

It’s a place where ‘I can be me’; true for a facilitator as much as for a student! Our Vision is to enable each child to develop a character that engages with the world with self-worth and acceptance of the other. Drawing inspiration from the philosophy of Integral Education integration of physical, mental, social, emotional and psychic self of children is emphasised. We believe that acceptance of the self and conflict resolution through dialogue opens pathways to peace.

Whats New?

  • Online session all of a sudden?

    To rekindle the light and warmth in this festive season, Children celebrated Diwali on this virtual platform with the same zeal and enthusiasm. Creating lanterns, garlands, puppets, rangolis, origami diyas and beautiful diya painting added imaginative colors to a festival celebration. Cleaning up the study area brought in the element of cleanliness and hygiene to the festivities. The virtual togetherness was felt by all while making besan laddoos, savoring their taste in entirety, and sharing the sweetness. Dressed up in ethnic attire and doing the Diwali Pujan together we all felt our inner lights shine as bright as the diyas beside.

  • How managed all this online shikshanter birthday disaster?

    The deep connection of Shikshantar community has always been displayed in its sense of growth, togetherness and love that shines and radiates energy to everything around! Assessing the readiness of our children at each step, being observant and mindful of their responses, identifying and acknowledging the challenges and together finding the answers; we took the journey of celebrating the Eighteenth Shikshantar Birthday on the virtual platform. Eighteenth year of Shikshantar - emotional maturity and a rounded and grounded sense of self, one that is progressive and is able to sustain independent thought and compassionate values. The theme for this year was 'f'k{kkarj vkSj ge'.Children of Primary School were immersed in experiences by connecting with their memories, people, and spaces in Shikshantar. ‘What does Shikshantar mean to me? ’, ‘Where would my feet want to go?’- Creative Writeups, poems, art expressions, riddles, gup – shup, and a lot more creatives came alive. Culmination to the theme and Shikshantar Birthday was scheduled on Monday, 28th of September 2020. Lighting the Diya together and celebrating it online with the Shikshantar family strengthened our belief in our philosophy.
